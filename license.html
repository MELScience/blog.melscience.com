If you see this page, then MEL Chemistry VR license is not applied.

1. Make sure you have MEL Chemistry VR installed.
Download the MEL Chemisty VR app on Google Play.

2. Open your license link.

3. In the system prompt, choose to open with MEL Chemistry VR.

4. Restart your Android device.

If this does not help, contact our support at support@melscience.com and we will help you.
